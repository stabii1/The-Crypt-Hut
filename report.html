Report Your Sighting

If you have a sighting you would like to report, there are a handful of ways to get in contact with us. Be sure to include important information, such as the date, time, and location of your sighting. Any photography or illustration of your cryptid is welcome. We thank you for your contribution.

  • Directly message us on our Discord through one of our moderators.
  • Send a ticket report in our Discord server.
  • Email us at thecrypthut@msn.com
